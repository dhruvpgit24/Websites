Travel Guidelines

Your safety and convenience is our priority

1. Travel Documentation

Ensure you have all required travel documents such as a valid ID, visa (if required), and booking confirmations.

2. Check Local Regulations

Always check the latest travel advisories, entry restrictions, and health protocols for your destination before your trip.

3. Pack Smart

Carry essentials like chargers, medications, and weather-appropriate clothing. Always keep a copy of important documents with you.

4. Health & Safety

Follow hygiene practices, stay hydrated, and avoid risky activities. Consider purchasing travel insurance for emergencies.

5. Booking Updates

Currently, we do not offer flight or hotel bookings, but these features will be available very soon on Skywings.
